## üèóÔ∏è **Architecture: The Value Exchange**

Instead of an Ad Server serving banners, the **Incentive Switchboard** serves **Offers & Rewards**.

### **The Participants:**

1.  **The Sponsor (Advertiser):**
    *   Wants: Emails, Signups, Beta Testers, Donors.
    *   Action: Deposits funds into an "Incentive Pool" (e.g., $10,000 budget).
    *   Rule: "Pay $1.00 for every verified signup."

2.  **The Host (Creator/Publisher):**
    *   Wants: To monetize traffic without annoying fans with ads.
    *   Action: Installs the **Incentive Badge** (The Lottery Widget).
    *   Benefit: Keeps users happy because they are winning stuff.

3.  **The User (Visitor):**
    *   Wants: Value, Status, Money.
    *   Action: Sees "You Won $1.00" or "Free Month of Premium".
    *   Benefit: Direct financial or utility gain.
